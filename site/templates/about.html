About us

Our mission

Our mission is to provide a high quality gaming experience to our community. This includes having reliable servers, a friendly community, and balanced gameplay. We do our best to achieve these objectives and we're always happy to receive feedback if you think something needs to be changed.

Next steps

Currently there are a few priorities on our radar. Firstly, we would like to launch a few more servers. Once we have a sizeable number of reliable and interesting servers to work with; we plan to focus on growing our community. On top of that, we also plan to continue updating and refining our website, and community tools.
