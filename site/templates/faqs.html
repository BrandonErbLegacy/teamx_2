Frequently Asked Questions page

This is where your questions about anything relating to the servers and staff can be found.

Do you have to pay to play on the servers?

No, you do not have to pay to be able to play on our servers. The servers are completely free.

Are there pay to win elements on the servers?

No, there are no pay to win elements on the servers we operate. We are commited to fair gameplay.

Are hacking and exploits allowed on the servers?

No, hacking is strictly banned. If you suspect someone hacking on our servers please report it to a staff member.

Can you request gameplay additions to any of the servers?

Yes, you can request gameplay additions by filing a ticket.

If there is a gameplay issue such as a bug do we inform a staff member?

Unless it is an urgent issue such as a imminent server crash, please file a ticket.

Are faction/PVP allowed?

Yes, if you would like to create a group with their own special discord category within the Team X discord server please contact one of the server admins.

We don't know!
